I am interested in, most generally, interdisciplinary math modeling, but particularly in areas with applications to psychiatry.

During my undergrad, I did research with professor Anh Tran on minimality of two-bridge knots. I also participated in the Georgia Tech's Math REU, working under professor Dan Margalit and Dr. Yvon Verberne on automorphism group of the fine curve graph.

Preprints and publications

Automorphisms of the fine curve graph (w/ Adele Long, Dan Margalit, Yvon Verberne, Claudia Yao) Accepted for publication at Transactions of the AMS (2021)

Minimality of rational knots C(2n+1, 2m, 2) (w/ Bradley Meyer and Anh T. Tran) Published in Journal of Knot Theory and Its Ramifications (2024)
